# MemoryCare — User Manual

A step-by-step guide to using the Memory Assistant System for each role. Replace
the *[screenshot]* markers with images from your running app.

## Getting started

1. Open the application at the web address (locally: `http://localhost:3000`).
2. On the landing page, choose **Login** or **Register**.
3. **Register** by entering your name, email, password, phone, and role
   (Patient or Caregiver). Admin accounts are created by the system
   administrator.
4. After logging in you are taken to the dashboard for your role.

## 1. Patient guide

After logging in, the patient sees a sidebar with all features. On a phone, tap
the **menu (☰)** button at the top-left to open the sidebar.

### 1.1 Dashboard
Shows today's progress — how many medicines and routines are done, and an
overall score. *[screenshot: patient dashboard]*

### 1.2 Medications
- View your prescribed medicines and their times.
- Tap **Mark as Taken** when you take a dose, or **Missed** if you did not.
- (Medicines are added by your caregiver.)

### 1.3 Routines
- See today's activities grouped by time of day (morning, afternoon, evening).
- Tap the circle to mark an activity **complete**.

### 1.4 Reminders (spoken)
- When a medicine or routine is due, a reminder appears at the top-right.
- Tap the **speaker button** in the top bar to have reminders **read aloud**.
- Choose your language in the chatbot; reminders speak in that language.

### 1.5 AI Chatbot
- Open **AI Chatbot**. Choose **EN** or **اردو** at the top.
- **Type** a question, or tap the **microphone 🎤** and **speak** it.
- Tap the **speaker 🔊** to have replies read aloud.
- Ask things like: *"What medicine do I take today?"*, *"What is my routine?"*,
  *"Who is my daughter?"*, *"What is the date?"*.
- **Clear Chat** starts a new conversation.

### 1.6 Face Recognition
- Open **Face Recognition** and allow camera access.
- **Add New Face:** upload a clear photo of a person and enter their name and
  relationship.
- **Scan:** press the capture button; the app shows and **says aloud** who the
  person is (e.g. *"This is Sarah, your daughter"*). Unknown faces are flagged.

### 1.7 Memory Gallery
- Browse photos of family, friends, and places with names and notes.
- Tap **Add Memory** to add a photo with people, place, date, and a description.

### 1.8 Reports
- Tap **Generate New Report**, choose a type and date range, and generate.
- Download any report as **PDF** or **Excel**.

### 1.9 SOS (emergency)
- The red **SOS** button (top bar) instantly alerts your caregiver. Use it only
  in an emergency; confirm when prompted.

### 1.10 Profile & Settings
- View your details, and change your password under **Settings**.

## 2. Caregiver guide

### 2.1 Dashboard
See your assigned patients with their medication/routine compliance and recent
alerts. *[screenshot: caregiver dashboard]*

### 2.2 Managing patients
- **Patients** lists the patients assigned to you. Open a patient to view their
  profile and details.

### 2.3 Medications & routines
- Add, edit, or delete **medications** and **routines** for an assigned patient.
- Changes appear immediately on the patient's screens.

### 2.4 Alerts
- View alerts (missed doses, SOS, unknown faces) for your patients.
- **Resolve** an alert once you have acted on it.

### 2.5 Notes & reports
- Add **care notes** for a patient.
- Generate and download patient **reports**.

> Access is limited: you can only see and manage patients assigned to you.

---

## 3. Administrator guide

### 3.1 Dashboard
System-wide statistics — users, patients, alerts, and overall health.

### 3.2 User management
View all users; deactivate or remove accounts as needed.

### 3.3 Contacts
Read messages submitted through the public **Contact** form.

## 4. Tips & requirements

- **Voice input** (microphone) works in **Chrome or Edge**.
- **Urdu speech output** needs an Urdu voice installed on the device
  (Windows: Settings → Time & Language → Language → add Urdu → Speech).
- **Camera** and **microphone** require permission; allow them when prompted.
- The app works on desktop and mobile screens.
